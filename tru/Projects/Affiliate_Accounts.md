## 2. What We Need From You (Account Setup Requirements)

To apply for and get approved by these tier-1 financial affiliate networks (Impact, CJ Affiliate, Fintel Connect), you must prepare the following business assets. **Financial networks are strict about compliance.**

### A. Business Entity Details
- **Legal Business Name** (or Sole Proprietor name)
- **Tax ID / EIN** (You will need to submit a W-9 form digitally during onboarding)
- **Business Address** & Phone Number

### B. Digital Assets (The "Property")
When applying, you must register `nh-financial-review` as your "Promotional Property".
- **Primary Domain URL**: The live URL of the listicle site.
- **Traffic Estimates**: Usually fine to put "Under 5,000 / month" for a new site, but emphasize "High-Intent Bottom-of-Funnel organic search traffic."
- **Verification Method**: You will need to either add a `<meta>` tag to the site's `<head>`, upload a `.txt` file to the root directory, or verify via email (`admin@yourdomain.com`). *I (Caleb) can handle the code verifications for you once you get them.*

### C. Financial / Payout Information
- **Bank Account Routing & Account Numbers** (For direct deposit of your commissions).
- **Payment Thresholds**: Decide on your minimum payout threshold (usually $50 or $100).
